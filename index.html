OUR DESTINATIONS

Rajasthan

Rajasthan
known as "The Land of Kings," is a state in northern India famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and majestic palaces. It's home to magnificent forts, beautiful deserts, and colorful festivals.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh
known as "The Abode of Snow," is a northern Indian state in the Himalayas. It's famous for its stunning landscapes, hill stations like Shimla and Manali, and adventure activities like trekking, skiing, and paragliding.

Kasol

Kasol
often referred to as "Mini Israel of India," is a small village in the Parvati Valley of Himachal Pradesh. It's renowned for its scenic beauty, serene ambiance, and trekking trails. It's a popular destination for backpackers and nature lovers.

Assam

Assam
known as "The Land of Kings," is a state in northern India famous for its rich history, vibrant culture, and majestic palaces. It's home to magnificent forts, beautiful deserts, and colorful festivals.

Amritsar

Amritsar
also known as "The Holy City," is in the northern state of Punjab. It is home to the Golden Temple, the holiest shrine in Sikhism. The city is also known for its rich history, bustling bazaars, and delicious Punjabi cuisine.
